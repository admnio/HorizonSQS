Queues hold steady through dawn watch

Five queues remain in motion across three transports — one paused for upstream maintenance; throughput trends upward over the prior hour.

By Sunset Observer · prod-cluster-east · 04:22 UTC

The shift began at midnight without incident. By dawn, three thousand eight hundred and forty-seven jobs awaited their workers — a figure four hundred twelve lower than the prior hour, owing largely to faster turnover on the webhooks queue, which has cleared its overnight backlog ahead of schedule.

The indexing queue continues to lag, having accumulated forty-eight seconds of wait against an Elasticsearch endpoint that has returned a dozen 429 responses since 04:00 UTC. Operators on duty are advised to consult the rate-limit ledger on Page VII before requesting additional capacity.

A single queue — geocode — has been paused at the operator's discretion, pending coordination with the upstream Google Maps quota window. Eight workers will cease popping at the next loop iteration. Producers continue to enqueue; the volume now stands at three hundred twelve pending jobs.
